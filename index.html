Welcome to EATA 2027

The EATA 2027 Conference brings together researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers from across Europe and beyond. The conference provides a forum for presenting scientific research, exchanging technical knowledge, and fostering collaboration in asphalt and pavement technologies.

We look forward to welcoming you to the historic city of Antwerp, Belgium, for three days of cutting-edge sessions, workshops, and networking opportunities that will shape the future of pavement engineering.

About EATA 2027

The European Asphalt Technology Association (EATA) brings together the leading minds in road infrastructure, bitumen, and pavement science. The annual conference is the premier forum for the exchange of research findings, technical innovations, and policy developments shaping the future of asphalt technology across Europe and beyond.

EATA 2027 will take place in Antwerp, Belgium — a city at the crossroads of Europe's transport and logistics networks — from 8 to 10 June 2027. The programme will include keynote addresses, technical sessions, interactive workshops, an industry exhibition, and social events.

Agenda

The EATA 2027 Conference brings together researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers from across Europe and beyond. The conference provides a forum for presenting scientific research, exchanging technical knowledge, and fostering collaboration in asphalt and pavement technologies.

Agenda for 2027:

08 June 2027 – Tuesday
09 June 2027 – Wednesday
10 June 2027 – Thursday
17:00 – 19:00  |  We welcome you to a welcome dinner reception for Argus Bitumen and Asphalt Europa Conference 2027
17:00 – 19:00

Welcome Reception & Networking Drinks

Pre-conference welcome drinks reception for EATA 2027 delegates. An excellent opportunity to reconnect with colleagues and meet new contacts ahead of the opening day.

08:00 – 09:00

Registration & Morning Coffee

Delegate check-in, badge collection and networking over coffee.

09:00 – 09:20

Welcome Address & Opening Remarks

Opening address from the EATA 2027 Conference Chair and invited guest speaker.

Conference Chair, EATA 2027
09:20 – 10:10

Keynote: Infrastructure Demand and Asphalt Quality Standards in Europe

How is evolving infrastructure demand shaping specifications for asphalt quality in Europe? What innovations in mix design and performance standards can help meet sustainability and durability goals?

Keynote Speaker – TBC
10:10 – 11:00

Session 1: Procurement Change and Supply Security

How can producers and contractors collaborate to ensure consistent quality under tightening regulations and cost pressures? Panel discussion with road authorities, refiners and contractors.

Panellists from Finnish Transport Infrastructure Agency, Norwegian Public Roads Administration, Swedish Transport Authority
11:00 – 11:20

Morning Break & Networking

Refreshments served in the exhibition area.

11:20 – 12:10

Session 2: HSE, Technical Standards and Decarbonisation Levers

What are the key current issues related to HSE and technical aspects for bitumen suppliers? How will the new Construction Product Regulation affect the declaration of performance for bitumen?

Speakers from Eurobitume, Continental Bitumen (Colas), Ferrovial
12:10 – 13:30

Networking Lunch

Lunch served in the exhibition area — an opportunity to meet sponsors, exhibitors and fellow delegates.

13:30 – 14:20

Session 3: EU ETS — Impact on Refinery and Shipping Economics

How will rising EU ETS allowance prices reshape cross-European voyage economics and chartering strategies? What does the influx of newbuild vessels mean for fleet management, compliance, and cost competitiveness?

Speakers from Groupe ADP, Cargill, Skanska
14:20 – 15:10

Session 4: Global Trade Impacts and Supply Chain Resilience

What impact are rising container premiums and shipping bottlenecks having on bitumen trade flows? Which upcoming infrastructure projects in Africa and the Middle East will reshape demand patterns?

Panellists from Continental Bitumen, trading companies and logistics operators
15:10 – 15:30

Afternoon Break & Networking

Refreshments served in the exhibition area.

15:30 – 16:20

Session 5: Next-Generation Asphalt Technologies

How are European contractors and suppliers leveraging warm-mixed asphalt to meet sustainability targets? How is RAP integration evolving in Europe's high-spec markets?

Speakers from Skanska, Ferrovial, Cargill NV
16:20 – 17:00

Panel Discussion: Balancing Sustainability, Quality, Durability and Cost

Senior industry leaders debate the key tensions and opportunities facing the asphalt and pavement sector over the next five years.

Moderated panel discussion
19:00 – 22:00

Conference Dinner & Networking Evening

Official EATA 2027 conference dinner and evening networking event. Venue and details to be announced.

08:30 – 09:00

Morning Coffee & Networking

Informal networking over coffee ahead of Day 3 sessions.

09:00 – 09:50

Session 6: Diversified Applications and Performance-Led Mixes

Exploring new applications for asphalt and pavement materials including airfield, industrial and urban applications. How can performance-led mix design reduce whole-life costs?

Speakers from Groupe ADP and academic institutions
09:50 – 10:40

Session 7: Case Studies — Innovation on European Roads

Practical case studies from European road authorities and contractors showcasing successful implementation of new materials, methods and specifications.

Speakers from Norwegian Public Roads Administration and Swedish Transport Authority
10:40 – 11:00

Morning Break & Exhibition

Refreshments served in the exhibition area.

11:00 – 11:50

Session 8: Americas Supply Impact on European Markets

Rising Venezuelan crude imports combined with growing asphalt supply is expected to result in a growing glut — how does this reshape European trade flows and pricing strategies?

Market analysts and trading companies
11:50 – 12:40

Workshop: Future Specifications and Regulatory Outlook to 2030

An interactive workshop exploring how specifications and regulatory frameworks are expected to evolve through 2030, including the impact of EU Green Deal commitments on asphalt procurement.

Facilitated workshop — delegates invited to participate
12:40 – 13:40

Networking Lunch

Final conference lunch — consolidate contacts and plan follow-up meetings.

13:40 – 14:30

Closing Keynote: The Road Ahead — Asphalt in a Net-Zero Europe

A forward-looking keynote assessing the strategic outlook for asphalt and pavement technologies in the context of Europe's net-zero commitments and evolving infrastructure investment cycles.

Senior Industry Leader – TBC
14:30 – 15:00

Closing Remarks & Conference Close

Summary reflections from the Conference Chair. Announcement of EATA 2028 location. Farewell refreshments.

Conference Chair, EATA 2027

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations received more than 60 days before the conference will receive a full refund minus an administration fee. Cancellations received between 30 and 60 days prior will receive a 50% refund. Cancellations received less than 30 days before the conference are non-refundable. Substitutions may be made at any time at no charge.

Sponsorship Opportunities

Sponsoring EATA 2027 provides unrivalled access to senior decision-makers from across the asphalt, bitumen and road construction supply chain. Contact us to discuss tailored sponsorship packages.

🏆 Diamond Sponsorship

Premium brand visibility across all conference materials, website and signage. Dedicated exhibition stand in prime location. Speaking opportunity in the main plenary programme. Full-page advertisement in the conference proceedings. Complimentary delegate passes.

🏅 Gold Sponsorship

High-profile brand visibility across conference materials and website. Exhibition stand in prominent location. Advertisement in the conference proceedings. Complimentary delegate passes.

🥈 Silver Sponsorship

Brand visibility in conference materials and website. Half-page advertisement in conference proceedings. Complimentary delegate pass.

🤝 Session / Event Sponsorship
